About ISSUEPOST

ISSUEPOST is an online platform available to the citizens 24x7 to lodge their grievances to the public authorities on any subject related to service delivery. It is one of the most efficient portal that is connected to the social media platforms so that, Every Ministry and States have role-based access to this system.

ISSUEPOST also provides appeal facility to the citizens if they are not satisfied with the resolution by the Grievance Officer. After closure of grievance if the complainant is not satisfied with the resolution on government Offices and websites, he/she can provide the feedback on ISSUEPOST. If the rating is Poor the option to file an appeal is enabled on ISSUEPOST, with this positive pressure on government ISSUEPOST & the citizens can live peacefully in the society!
